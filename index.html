Project Owners

Get on board

In a few minutes, register an account with your personal information. Once an account has been created for you, set up your company in a quick onboarding process.

Setup new project

Create a project in your account, upload a pitch deck and specify your funding goal. Your submission will be pending until it has been reviewed.

Wait for feedback

The team will review your submission against our guidelines and reach out to you if we require further details. Once approved, investors can fund your project.
